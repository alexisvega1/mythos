Once upon a time, a small fox lived in the forest.
The fox liked to run and play near the river every morning.
Birds sang in the trees while the sun rose over the hills.
A friendly dog came to visit and they shared a warm meal.
The rain fell softly on the green leaves in the quiet woods.
Children laughed as they built a tiny house from sticks and stones.
The old owl watched from a branch and hooted into the night.
Stars appeared in the sky and the world felt calm and safe.
A little boat floated on the lake under the bright moon.
The cat slept on a soft rug by the warm fire in the cottage.
